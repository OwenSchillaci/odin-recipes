Chicken Pot Pie

Description

Every home cook needs a good chicken pot pie in their repertoire. You're in luck! This homemade chicken pot pie deserves a permanent spot in your recipe box.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Vegetables
  • Butter and Flour
  • Seasonings
  • Broth and Milk

Steps

  1. Make the Filling. Boil the cubed chicken with the vegetables for about 15 minutes.
  2. Cook onions in butter until they're translucent, then stir in the flour and seasonings. Add the chicken broth and milk and simmer until the filling is thick.
  3. Fill and Bake the Pie. Place the chicken and vegetables mixture in an unbaked pie crust. Pour the chicekn broth mixture over it. Buke until the patry is golden brown and filling is bubbly.
